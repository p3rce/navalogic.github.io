Our Team

Meet the executive members of the Navalogic family

Ganesh Navaratnarjah

Entrepreneur, Business Consultant and Founder of Navalogic

Ganesh Navaratnarjah was the original founder of Navalogic and has over 20 years of business consulting experience. He holds a degree in Aerospace Engineering from Toronto Metropolitan University. Ganesh has worked for several companies ranging from Engineering, Pharma and Financial institutions.

Ganesh's 'self-starter' mentality led to the creation of the NavaCup initiative. The annual golf tournament raises funds for at risk youth; and provides them with laptops to succeed in their studies (navacup.org). Bridging the digital divide has always been one of Ganesh's goals. To date, NavaCup has donated close to 400 laptops to at risk youth where 95%+ have gone on to post secondary education.

Ganesh is also the president of the executive board of the Boys and Girls Clubs of East Scarborough (BGCES). He prides himself on giving back to the community. In addition to the NavaCup & the BGCES, Ganesh has volunteered time in Ghana (west Africa) as well as Tanzania; to most recently teach business at a school dedicated to educate and empower women. "You get by giving back" is a key mantra that Navalogic will always stand for.

Jeremy Hayward

Entrepreneur, Business Advisor and Digital Strategist

Jeremy Hayward is a seasoned Digital Strategist with over 20 years in the Information Technology sector, known for transforming business operations through digital innovation. He has notably served as the President and owner of a technology firm with 60 employees, showcasing his leadership and expertise in driving growth and technological advancement. Jeremy is well-versed in guiding businesses towards maximized digital efficiency.

His involvement in initiatives like the Ozone Advisory Chair Circle and ConnectWise Evolve Groups reflects his dedication to ongoing industry evolution. In addition to his corporate roles, Jeremy is experienced in board advisory, business advisory, strategic planning, and governance. He is co-founder of the GrandBay-Westfield Business Group, demonstrating his commitment to business community development, and has held significant positions such as Vice Chair of the Edmonton Downtown Business Association and a member of the Downtown Coalition Task Force, reflecting his dedication to urban business and community collaboration.

Jeremy is also engaged in philanthropy, supporting various non-profits. His diverse experience underscores his leadership in business innovation and community involvement.

Michael Wallace

Business Advisor, Speaker and Coach

Michael is a strategic consultant at Navalogic, a business advisory company with a focus on creating real behavioral change in leaders and their teams through advising, coaching and training. He is a highly effective facilitator, coach, speaker, and author of the book Motorcycle Metaphor: Leadership Lessons for the Progressive Organization. Michael has provided training consultation and facilitation and has been a keynote speaker for some of North America's largest organizations, including Pratt & Whitney, Novartis, CN Rail, Phillips.

Michael has over 20 years of progressive pharmaceutical experience and has held roles in pharmaceutical sales, medical communications, operations and training and development, where his passion for purposeful and authentic learning led to the establishment of Fascinnovation and a decade serving as a faculty member at the Canadian Management Centre; a leader in professional development, organizational communication, and leadership training.

Michael has collaborated with multiple international clients to design, develop, and facilitate programs in areas such as sales execution, leadership development, effective communication, coaching skills, and team development. With a collaborative and inclusive leadership style he brings managers and teams together helping them achieve the business objectives. As an experienced and certified facilitator and coach his interactive and experiential programs are highly sought after and have been successful in improving collaboration and communication amongst attendees.

Tim Holmes

Entrepreneur/Digital Transformation/Leadership/Love to Inspire!

Tim is a senior strategic advisor and partner with Navalogic bringing over 25 years of senior IT leadership experience in areas such as Infrastructure, Systems, DevOps, Cloud, Data Center, IT Security coupled with 20 years of Senior IT leadership. Tim has led teams with up to 60 direct reports through Private, Public, Government (union and non-union) sectors Internationally from start-up through to enterprise environments. He brings a customer centric, collaborative approach to making a positive valued impact to each business and personal relationship.

Tim has designed and deployed multiple multiyear IT/IS strategy digital transformation roadmaps for small, medium and enterprise organizations and brings strengths in the areas of Strategic Alignment, Value Delivery, Risk Management, Resource and Budget Management coupled with leading Organizations IT security practices.

He has led large in scope and impacting projects for numerous organizations throughout North America over the past 25+ years such as ERP migrations/implementations, Microsoft 365 deployments, IT Security/Risk assessments and roadmaps built within overall IT/IS multiyear roadmaps, back up and disaster recovery planning and deployment (Senior Leader for natural disasters, Fort McMurray Fires, Calgary Flood).
